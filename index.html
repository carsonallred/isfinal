Ironman Triathlon

I’m passionate about training for and racing in Ironman 70.3 and full-distance races. It’s a challenging but incredibly rewarding journey that pushes me physically and mentally. Here’s what goes into it:

  1. Core Disciplines
    • Swimming – open water and pool sessions
    • Cycling – long rides and interval training
    • Running – tempo runs, long runs, and brick workouts

  2. Recovery & Injury Prevention
    • Stretching and mobility work
    • Sleep and active recovery days
    • Massage guns, foam rollers, and compression

  3. Nutrition & Fueling
    • Daily balanced meals to support training
    • Pre-race carb loading and hydration
    • On-course fueling strategy (gels, sports drinks, etc.)

  4. Mindset & Strategy
    • Race pacing plans
    • Mental toughness in long sessions
    • Goal setting and race season planning
